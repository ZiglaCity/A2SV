# A2SV Journey

This repository tracks my A2SV learning journey, contest practice, and phase-based progress.

## How I Entered A2SV

I applied at the end of my Level 100 year.

I was interviewed on Friday, Jan 10, 2025, from 12:30 PM to 2:00 PM (East Africa Time).

The interview had:

- One technical problem (a two-pointer problem)
- One behavioral section

A recorded session of the full interview will be uploaded as an unlisted YouTube video soon.

## First Education Phase (In-Person)

This phase took place at the University of Ghana.

During the first period, we covered:

- Python basics
- Lists and arrays
- Sets
- Other foundational data structures

## First Camping Phase

During the camping phase, we covered:

- Sliding window
- Prefix sum
- Linked list
- Stacks
- Queues
- Recursion

This phase eventually ended without prior notice.

## Transition to G7 (Remote)

After that, we were told to apply for the G7 remote phase, and some of us did.

I got called for another interview on Sunday, Dec 14, 2025, from 7:30 AM to 9:00 AM (Greenwich Mean Time - Abidjan).

That interview had two technical problems:

- One easy
- One medium

The full process did not even last 30 minutes.

Now I am in remote education with fellows across Africa. As of now, we are done with recursion.
